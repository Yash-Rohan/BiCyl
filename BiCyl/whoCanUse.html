How to Use the BiCyl

Step 1: Registration

To use the cycle rental system, you must first register an account via the website.

  1. Go to the cycle rental system's website.
  2. Click on "Register" or "Sign Up".
  3. Fill in your details, such as your name, email, phone number, and password.
  4. Set up a payment method (credit/debit card, cash, or digital wallet).
  5. Verify your account via a confirmation email or SMS.

Step 2: Locate a Cycle

After registering, you can locate a nearby cycle by visiting the map section on the website:

  • Log in to your account on the website.
  • Go to the "Map" or "Stations" section to view available cycles at nearby stations.
  • Choose the station with available cycles that is closest to you.
  • Go to the station to pick up your cycle.

Step 3: Rent a Cycle

Once you're at the station, follow these steps to rent a cycle using the website:

  1. Log in to your account on the website from your mobile device or computer.
  2. Find the cycle you want to rent at the station and enter its identification number on the website.
  3. Click "Unlock" on the website, and the system will unlock the cycle automatically. Alternatively, you may receive a code to enter on the cycleâ€™s keypad to unlock it.
  4. Your rental will start once the cycle is unlocked.

Step 4: During Your Ride

During your ride, follow these safety and usage guidelines:

  • Wear a helmet for safety.
  • Follow all traffic rules and signals.
  • Use dedicated bike lanes whenever available.
  • If you need to stop during your ride, some systems allow you to temporarily lock the cycle via the website.

Step 5: Return the Cycle

When your ride is finished, return the cycle to any nearby docking station:

  1. Find the nearest docking station by checking the "Map" section of the website.
  2. Return the cycle to an empty dock and securely lock it.
  3. Log in to the website and confirm that the cycle has been returned by clicking on "End Ride".
  4. Your rental period will end, and the system will calculate your rental charge based on the ride duration.

Step 6: Payment

After returning the cycle, the system will automatically process your payment. You can check your ride history and view your charges on the website in the "My Account" or "Ride History" section.

Step 7: Additional Features

The cycle rental system may offer extra features on the website, such as:

  • Monthly or annual membership plans for frequent users.
  • Discounted multi-ride passes.
  • Real-time cycle availability notifications.
