Who Can Use This Service?

BiCyl is a service designed specifically for the students, faculty, and staff of the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK). To be eligible to use this service, users must meet the following criteria:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Current Students: All students currently enrolled at NITK, both undergraduate and postgraduate, are eligible to use this service.
  • Faculty Members: Teaching staff and faculty members at NITK can also use the cycle rental service for commuting across campus.
  • Staff Members: Administrative and support staff employed by NITK are welcome to use this service.
  • Valid NITK ID: All users must have a valid NITK student or employee ID card to register for and access the cycle rental system.

Note: External visitors and non-NITK individuals are not eligible to use the cycle rental service. This service is exclusively for NITK community members.
